Construction Tender

Africa's infrastructure construction demands are substantial and growing, with the African Development Bank estimating that the continent needs to invest $130-170 billion annually to address the infrastructure gap. This need is driven by rapid urbanization, a burgeoning population, and the desire for economic growth. However, a significant funding gap of up to $100 billion per year hinders progress.

Africa faces a huge infrastructure deficit, particularly in areas like energy, transportation, and water supply and sanitation. Other areas include roads, railways, ports, energy, housing and telecommunications.
