Laboratory Equipment & Services Tender

COVID-19 pandemic exposed the need to have modern laboratories, and spurred increased self-reliance and collaboration within the African laboratory landscape.

Governments and private sector investment in healthcare infrastructure are boosting the demand for laboratory equipment. There is constant need for accurate diagnostics. Sophisticated diagnostic tools are crucial for timely disease detection and treatment, leading to better healthcare outcomes.

East Africa Countries like Sudan, Uganda are experiencing rapid growth and are becoming increasingly important in the global laboratory equipment market.

There's a significant opportunity to improve healthcare in areas with limited access to diagnostic tools and laboratory facilities.

The African laboratory sector, particularly in IVD (in vitro diagnostics) and laboratory medicine, is experiencing significant growth and is projected to reach $1.78 billion by 2029.
