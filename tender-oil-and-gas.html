Oil & Gas Tender

Africa is a major player in the global oil and gas sector, possessing significant reserves and production. While the continent produces a substantial amount of crude oil, it consumes a relatively small portion and exports a large share.

However, the industry faces challenges, including global price volatility, the need for local value addition, and the impact of energy transitions.

East Africa is emerging as an exciting prospect for international oil companies, with significant investment potential in the coming years. There is a growing focus on promoting local content development and value addition in the oil and gas sector, including refining and petrochemicals, to reduce reliance on imported products.

Our group will leverage on its market experience and networks to find your business government contracts for oil and gas tenders, global oil tenders, global gas tenders, drilling tenders, fuel tenders, lubricant tenders, international oil gas tenders, natural gas tenders, Transformers, cables, meters, poles, petrol tenders, diesel tenders, lpg tenders, oilfield tenders, petrochem tenders, refinery tenders, onshore tenders, offshore tenders.
