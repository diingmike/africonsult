Water & Sanitation Tender

The water and sanitation sector in Africa faces significant challenges, with millions lacking access to safe water, sanitation, and hygiene services. Africa needs substantial investment, estimated at $55-$66 billion annually, to achieve universal access. Initiatives like the African Water Facility (AWF) and the Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Initiative (RWSSI) are working to address these challenges.

Millions of people lack basic drinking water, sanitation, and hygiene services, particularly in rural areas. A major infrastructure deficit exists, requiring significant investment to improve water and sanitation systems.
