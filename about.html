About Us

We are a group of consultants with specialization in helping foreign businesses navigate the tender process, with a focus on opportunities and resources within the African continent, especially East Africa.

As a group, we offer consulting and tender preparation services within East African Countries (Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Rwanda, Mozambique, Sudan & Burundi). Our group assists foreign suppliers, contractors, with navigating the complex world of tenders, ensuring compliance and increasing the chances of successful bid submissions. We assist foreign contractors and suppliers to successfully participate in tender processes.

We work closely with Regional Governments, Country Governments, International Relief Agencies, Humanitarian Agencies.
