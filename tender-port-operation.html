Port Operation Tender

The Port of Mombasa, Kenya is a major hub for shipping and cargo handling, with various services like mooring and berthing. Ports Authority actively involved in developing new infrastructure, including small ports and inland waterways, to promote fishing and regional trade.

The Tanzania Ports Authority handles over 90% of Tanzania's international trade. It also serves as a crucial gateway for landlocked countries like Malawi, Zambia, DRC, Rwanda, Burundi, and Uganda. The government has invested heavily in upgrading infrastructure and increasing port capacity. While Dar es Salaam is a major port, its capacity is being stretched, further upgrades and investments are needed to improve infrastructure.

Dar es Salaam port is currently undergoing a major expansion and modernization to double its cargo capacity, creating opportunities for infrastructure development, logistics, and related service.

There is need for new wide range of port equipment such as reach stacker, container handler; Forklift and Reach Truck, material handler, terminal tractors, reachstackers and large port cranes like ship-to-shore container crane, portal jib crane, rail-mounted gantry crane, rubber tyre gantry crane, Rail cranes, Ship-to-shore cranes, Container cranes, Straddle carriers, Container Handler, Side handlers.
